❓ FAQ

Q. Which MBTI type is the most loyal?
Introverted intuitive types like INTJ, INFJ and INFP top the loyalty ranking — once they choose someone, they stay devoted and rarely stray.
Q. Which MBTI type is best for marriage?
Responsible, stability-loving types such as ISTJ, ISFJ and ESTJ rank highest for marriage and are great at building a lasting home.

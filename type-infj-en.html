The idealist who loves one person deeply

You read your crush perfectly, but you alone cannot say your true feelings. You just want one person to understand.

Personality

Gentle and thoughtful, this one reads the subtleties of feeling with startling sensitivity. Its ideals are high, and though it hides it, a fierce, unshakable conviction lives deep inside. Rather than wide acquaintance, it prefers deep bonds with a precious few, and its quiet way of standing beside another in pain is its charm. Behind those soft eyes a lot is turning, and at times it bottles up even its own emotions. Quiet on the surface, it is deeply strong at the core.

In Love

In love it has a romantic streak that seeks fate and a deep connection. It rarely dates casually, opening its heart only slowly after carefully gauging the other persons inner world and values. So once it believes in someone, it loves them deeply and loyally, as if giving its whole self. Perceptive, it reads even unspoken feelings and is kind, yet it struggles to show its own true feelings or weakness and tends to carry them alone. When understood, it can finally relax and entrust its heart.

When You Date

They build a quiet but deep bond that connects heart to heart. They notice your smallest changes and gently stand by you. When they feel safe, they pour out the love they kept inside.

When You Fight

They hide emotion and tend to shoulder it alone, not speaking until their limit. Do not interrogate; reassure them "you can talk to me" and they finally share their true feelings.

How to Get Closer

Do not rush. More than surface excitement, what matters most is taking time until it feels it can tell you its true feelings. Listen without interrupting and show that you share its values, and the firmly closed door opens little by little. It is drawn less by looks or status than by the moment it feels an inner connection. And turn your attention to this ever-attentive one too: gently ask what it wants, and it feels deeply fulfilled, treasuring you as the only one.

The caring crowd-pleaser who loves out loud

Personality

Sociable and caring, a beloved figure liked by everyone. Skilled at reading the room and never short on attentiveness, it finds joy in making people smile. Friendly and approachable to all, it is often at the center of the group. Bright and energetic, it treasures human connection above all. On the other hand, it cares quite a bit about how others see it and has a touching, kind side that overdoes things out of a wish not to be disliked.

In Love

Its love is straightforward and unstinting. It pours plenty of affection on the one it loves and diligently treasures anniversaries and small considerations. It values time together and frequent communication most, with a slightly clingy side that hopes for the same amount of love in return. Good at giving and dependable, yet when its partners reactions are faint or contact goes quiet, it grows anxious and stores up loneliness. It gets jealous sometimes, but that is proof of how loyally it cares. This is one who makes you feel warm just being together.

When You Date

Straightforward, unstinting affection. Diligent with anniversaries and contact, so you feel deeply loved. Being a lonely heart, frequent reassurance keeps them steady.

When You Fight

Fearing being disliked, they get emotional easily and may blame from loneliness. Reassure first with "I love you," and they calm down and meet you halfway.

How to Get Closer

The key to reassuring this one is frequent contact and words of thanks. Just saying thank you and I love being with you fills its heart and makes it devote itself to you all the more. Receiving its love firmly and returning the same amount matters most; faint reactions make it anxious, so take care. Gently tend its lonely side and tell it you are number one, and the distance closes fast. Answer its abundant love with equal warmth, and this one cherishes you without holding back.

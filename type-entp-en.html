The thrill-seeking free spirit who never bores you

Personality

Quick-witted and a smooth talker who livens up any room, this one is a crowd favorite. Mad for novelty and fun, it hates sitting still and is always hunting the next thrill. A creative idea machine, it loves debate and a bit of wordplay. Light on its feet, it acts the moment it gets an idea. With that energy and freedom, it keeps everyone around it excited. It may get bored easily, but no one beats its curiosity.

In Love

Boredom is its greatest enemy. It dislikes ruts and predictable routines, always craving fresh stimulation and a little thrill. It enjoys the chase and would rather pursue than be pursued. It closes in on a crush with witty banter, but if you are too easy to win, its interest can cool in an instant. On the other hand, someone who reveals a side it did not expect, or who can spar with it intellectually, makes it fall for real. It loves freedom and dislikes being tied down, yet around someone it trusts it shows a surprisingly loyal face.

When You Date

A relationship full of stimulation and surprises that never bores. All-in on talk and play, never dull to be with. Ruts are the enemy, so keeping it fresh is the key to lasting.

When You Fight

They half-enjoy a good debate and may out-talk you. But they do not hold grudges and forget quickly. Answer with humor and you patch things up surprisingly easily.

How to Get Closer

The key to winning this one is, simply, never letting it get bored. Offer unpredictable turns and new experiences to share, and its eyes light up with fascination. It also adores a good back-and-forth of clever conversation, so quick comebacks score points. The trick, though, is not to reveal everything: leave a little mystery. Make it think there is still so much it does not know about you, and this born chaser will close the distance on its own.

🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 INFP · Mediator

The romantic who guards an ideal love

Breed：Maltese

You dream of an ideal love and quietly ache at the gap with reality. But once you fall, you are straight and deep.

Personality

Gentle and delicate, with a vast, beautiful ideal world inside. Highly sensitive, it treasures its own values and its sense of who it wants to be above all. Drawn to daydreams, stories, and the artistic, it seems softly dreamy, yet within it holds an uncompromising core. It dislikes conflict and is easily affected by the mood around it and by others feelings, which gives it a deep kindness that can quietly stand beside someones pain.

In Love

A pure, romantic soul who dreams of a destined partner. Its ideals are clear, and it deeply treasures flutter and heart-to-heart connection. Once it falls, it is straightforward and devoted; it naturally wants to give for the other and its feelings run deep and last. On the other hand, prone to inflating ideals in its head, it can be quietly hurt by the gap with reality. It struggles to put true feelings into words and sometimes endures while keeping them inside. The defenseless way it lets itself be doted on, shown only to someone safe, is irresistibly sweet.

When You Date

A romantic, devoted partner who treasures their ideals. They naturally give for you and cherish a world that is just the two of you. Accepted without judgment, they let themselves be defenselessly doted on.

When You Fight

Sensitive, they go quiet and close their heart when blamed, tending to endure without voicing it. Ask gently and say "I am on your side," and they feel safe enough to meet you halfway.

How to Get Closer

What matters most is accepting this ones world and sensibility without denying any of it. Empathize with an I get it, and once it feels you are a safe place, it will slowly confide the feelings it kept inside. Do not rush it or shatter its dreams with too much cold logic. It is also weak to gentle words and little surprises: romantic touches that reach the heart. Make it feel it can be exactly itself with you, and it returns a straightforward, deep love without holding back.

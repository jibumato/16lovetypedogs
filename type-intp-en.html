🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 INTP · Logician

The deep-diving seeker who falls hard once hooked

Breed：Miniature Schnauzer

You overthink "is this love?" until you cannot move. Then you fall, and become more loyal than anyone.

Personality

A ball of curiosity and inquiry, with a head perpetually full of thoughts: the philosopher type. Easygoing, it dives deep into whatever fascinates it while being startlingly indifferent to anything it finds pointless. With its own angles and logic, it strikes people as a bit odd but interesting. Showing emotion is not its strong suit, and it keeps friendships narrow and deep. Rather than lively crowds, this one shines in quiet conversations with someone it clicks with.

In Love

It tends to turn even romance into something to analyze. While it ponders whether this is love or just curiosity, it often misses the crucial moment. Its approach is shy and clumsy, but once it concludes that this is the one, its affection runs deep and true. It dislikes being tied down and values a distance where both worlds are respected. It says little, yet it observes you closely and shows a kindness that hits the heart at unexpected moments.

When You Date

A comfortable bond where freedom is mutually respected, no clinginess. Few words, but they show love through small thoughtfulness and shared discoveries. They last with someone they can sit in silence with.

When You Fight

Instead of clashing emotionally, they try to analyze the cause coolly, sometimes retreating into their own world. Do not corner them with logic; give them time to think and they come back on their own.

How to Get Closer

Rather than pushing with emotion, conversation that sparks its intellect is the surest route. With someone it can think alongside and share discoveries with, it opens up naturally. Slow replies or flat reactions are not disinterest; it is simply choosing its words carefully in its head. Do not rush it; respect its pace. Make it feel that being with you is easy and fun, and you win. Before it knows it, it is the one who cannot let you go.

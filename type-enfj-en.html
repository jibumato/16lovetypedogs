🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 ENFJ · Protagonist

Everyone's sun, devoted and adored

Breed：Golden Retriever

Their happiness is your happiness. You give so much that, before you know it, you put yourself last.

Personality

Bright and caring, a charismatic sunshine type that people naturally gather around. Skilled at reading feelings, it warms the room and is a born mood-maker who makes everyone smile. It cannot leave anyone in trouble alone and cannot help but look after others. Sociable and friendly with all, it actually cares a great deal about others opinions and expectations, and has an earnest side that quietly shoulders a lot while putting on a brave face.

In Love

Deeply loving and crazy about giving, this is a romantic at heart. The other persons happiness is its own; for someone it loves it acts naturally and pours out affection. Popular and sociable, yet toward the one it loves it is straightforwardly loyal. It never forgets anniversaries or small considerations and cherishes its partner completely. But it can give so much that it puts itself last, and when the same love is not returned it quietly stores up loneliness without a word. Loved by many, it is sometimes misunderstood, this affection-filled one.

When You Date

An affection-filled partner who devotes themselves and treasures you, never missing anniversaries or care. Since they over-give, returning love reassures them.

When You Fight

Wanting to avoid conflict, they tend to endure and smooth things over, bottling up feelings. Draw them out with "what do you want?" and the relationship deepens.

How to Get Closer

This one is always on the giving side, so noticing the love you receive and returning it clearly in words and actions is what resonates most. Tell it thank you and that it deserves to be cherished too, and the tension in its heart loosens as it feels you are someone special. Even when it acts cheerful, it is on guard inside, so becoming a safe person it can voice its worries to closes the gap fast. Grow a warm relationship where you both smile, and this one will love you with all it has.

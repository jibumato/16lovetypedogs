Personality

A bundle of drive, the curious and energetic type. Its body moves before it thinks: a genius of vibe and momentum. It loves novelty and thrilling experiences and hates sitting still. Light on its feet, it warms up anywhere instantly and is a mood-maker who sweeps others up. It can charge ahead without much thought, but its straightforward brightness and drive keep company from ever being bored. Full of energy and impossible to dislike: a beloved one.

In Love

See someone it likes and it approaches at once. A passionate, straightforward romantic that values speed and vibe, it closes in boldly on a crush and deepens love by sharing exciting experiences. Rather than mulling things over, it would rather move and find out, so the relationship progresses quickly too. Craving stimulation and fun, it is weak to ruts and can cool toward a dull relationship. But with someone it truly clicks with, it builds a natural, lasting bond, laughing together like playmates. A partner who never gets old and is the most fun to be with.

When You Date

An active, easygoing partner who is never boring. They act on impulse and keep dates lively. They hate boredom, so enjoying things together is the secret to lasting.

When You Fight

They clash straight on the spot but do not hold back or sulk afterward, and dislike nagging. Have it out cleanly and reset with something fun, and you are back to normal fast.

How to Get Closer

The key to winning this one is good tempo and a lets-enjoy-it-together attitude. React with good vibes, get excited together, and dive into active dates and new experiences, and the distance closes fast. It prefers straightforward, clear exchanges over roundabout games, so conveying your liking honestly is the right move. Since not boring it matters most, keep sharing times you can both laugh in and little thrills. Let it think you are someone it can be excited with, and this one drives straight toward you with its trademark momentum.

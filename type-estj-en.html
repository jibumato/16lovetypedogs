The sincere gentleman who builds love steadily

Personality

A solid organizer, realistic and great at logistics, the dependable leader type. Skilled at moving things along by plan, it cannot stand vagueness or irresponsible behavior. It values doing things properly and earns trust as the dependable, no-nonsense one. Just like its tidy, tuxedo-clad look, it is a courteous, sincere gentleman. Caring and firmly supportive of those close, it can sometimes be too hard on itself and others: an earnest one indeed.

In Love

Its love is planned and sincere. It dislikes dragging out a vague situation; if it dates, it wants to face things seriously, with an eye on the future. It shows its intentions to a crush clearly and, once decided, grows the relationship straight and steadily. More than surprises or passionate displays, it expresses love by keeping promises and stacking up stable days: a steady type. A bit bossy and stubborn, but that is the flip side of a sense of responsibility, of cherishing and wanting to protect its partner. It makes a sincere, dependable, principled partner.

When You Date

A dependable partner who builds the relationship by plan and with the future in mind. Once decided, they go straight ahead and protect you firmly. A bit stiff, so make relaxed time together.

When You Fight

They insist on what is right and state it clearly, with a stubborn streak. Acknowledge their feelings then talk calmly rather than countering with logic, and they find a compromise.

How to Get Closer

For this one, showing a clear attitude and genuine intent is the greatest reassurance. Hedging or treating promises lightly drops your standing fast, so facing it straight and sincerely is key. It is drawn to proper, principled people, so having your own thoughts and a vision for the future scores well. A bit stiff, it will show its natural face if you spend relaxed, easygoing time together now and then. Stack up trust, and it returns a loyal, sincere love more dependable than anyone.

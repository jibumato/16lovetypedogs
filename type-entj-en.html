🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 ENTJ · Commander

The take-charge leader of love

Breed：Corgi

When you fall, you want to lead with everything. You thrive on being needed, but you just cannot show weakness.

Personality

A born leader who heads straight for the goal. Quick to decide and brimming with drive, this is a dependable one that naturally pulls others along. Skilled at taking charge, it ends up at the center of any group. Competitive and hardworking, with sky-high ambition, it cannot stand stagnation or excuses. Just like its proud stance on short legs, its refusal to fret over little things is charming. That said, its perfectionism can make it hard on itself and others.

In Love

In love it wants to hold the reins, designing the dates and the pace of the relationship itself. It approaches a crush directly and with a plan, and dislikes letting a vague situation drag on. With a strong urge to protect and support, once it decides this is the one it leads with everything and gives generously. It thrives on being relied upon, so it is wonderfully dependable. But its pride makes showing weakness hard, so it often shoulders things alone when it really wants to be doted on.

When You Date

They lead the relationship fully, planning dates and the future and pulling you along. A strong urge to protect, generous in giving. They love it when you let them dote on you.

When You Fight

They want black and white and may push with logic. But really they just want to make up. Do not deny their feelings; once you say "maybe you are right," their attitude softens fast.

How to Get Closer

This one delights in being relied upon, so leaning on it and bringing it your worries works well. Say honestly that you feel safe with it, and it will redouble its efforts to support you. On top of that, gently catch the rare moments of weakness and true feeling it shows, and the cuteness beneath the stern leader mask quickly draws you closer. A relationship of equals who can speak their minds is welcome too. More than flattery, it is people with a clear center of their own who capture this ones heart.

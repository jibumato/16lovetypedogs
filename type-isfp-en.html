🐾 Love Pedigree 🐾 ISFP · Adventurer

The free-spirited healer who loves at their pace

Breed：French Bulldog

You want to stay easygoing and natural. Pressure makes you want to flee, but with someone safe you let yourself be doted on.

Personality

Mild and easygoing, a healing type that treasures its own sensibility and comfort above all. Disliking conflict, it values enjoying this very moment calmly. It looks loose and free, but in fact it has rich aesthetic sense and sensitivity and holds firm preferences of its own. It may seem to go with the flow, yet it has a core it will not yield. It wears a warm, peaceful air that makes your shoulders relax just by being together.

In Love

A type that prefers an easygoing, natural love. It dislikes heavy moods, being tied down, and frantic relationships, and as a peace-lover it wants to grow love leisurely with someone comfortable to be around. Rather than pressing passionately, it deepens affection bit by bit through calm time spent together. Gentle and a touch easily swayed, it often does not state its true feelings clearly. It is thought hard to read, but that is the kindness of not wanting to make waves. Before someone safe, it peeks out a defenseless, doting face.

When You Date

A calm, natural, soothing partner you relax with. They prefer a free, easygoing bond. When they feel safe, they get defenselessly clingy—an adorable gap.

When You Fight

Disliking conflict, they tend to dodge the moment or go silent, finding it hard to voice feelings. Do not rush; in a safe atmosphere, gently draw them out and they meet you halfway.

How to Get Closer

Rushing or applying pressure is off-limits. For this one, creating a relaxed, comfortable atmosphere is the surest route. Let it feel calm being with you, and the distance closes naturally. Bad at voicing true feelings, it helps not to push but to gently draw them out. For its indecisive side too, it feels reassured if you organize the options and stand by it without rushing. Respect a calm, free relationship while letting it be doted on just enough, and this one will come to feel that being beside you is the comfiest place of all.

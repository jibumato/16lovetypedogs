Personality

Curious and expressive, a beloved mood-maker forever hunting for fun. Friendly to a fault, it warms up to anyone instantly and wraps the room in bright energy. Its feelings show plainly; it rejoices with its whole body and dives headlong into fun, and that innocence is its charm. It loves novelty and stimulation with broad interests, but can be a touch fickle and moody. With a free, colorful sensibility, this one gives you energy just by being there.

In Love

A passionate, straightforward type who enjoys love with everything it has. Wonderfully expressive, once it falls it does not hide its feelings and appeals directly. It treasures flutter and excitement above all and longs for a relationship it can be thrilled in together. Fun and easy to be with, yet it loves freedom and dislikes being tied down. And lively as it seems, it is actually a lonely heart that grows anxious when it cannot regularly feel a loved ones affection. Its moods swing, but at its core it is loyal and pours straightforward love on the one it trusts.

When You Date

A bright, passionate partner who makes every day fun, sharing feelings straight without hiding. They value freedom yet, being a lonely heart, want frequent affection.

When You Fight

Emotions show easily, so they clash on the spot—but they do not dwell and want to make up fast. Empathize and one hug brings the smile right back.

How to Get Closer

Whether you can share that this is fun with this one is the biggest point. React big, get excited together, and color its days with new experiences and surprises, and its eyes shine with devotion. Not taking away its flutter and freedom is the secret to lasting, but at the same time, tend gently to its lonely side. Frequent messages and a straightforward you are number one melt its anxiety away. Do not get too heavy, but do convey your love firmly: that balance captures this ones heart.

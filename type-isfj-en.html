The healing giver who devotes quietly

Breed：Shih Tzu

You show it through action, not words. You give quietly, but really you wish they would notice.

Personality

Calm and full of consideration, the quiet pillar who gently supports those around. It notices small changes and slight off-days in people and has the warmth to reach out unobtrusively. Rather than stepping forward, it is good at watching over the whole from a step back, and many are soothed by its reassuring presence. Modest and reserved, yet its wish to protect loved ones is stronger than anyone, this devoted one hides a principled kindness.

In Love

With someone it loves, it gives quietly but completely. Pushing forward is not its thing, but it observes its partner closely and shows affection with care that scratches right where it itches. It hopes for a stable, gentle relationship and, once it loves someone, has the loyalty to keep standing beside them. But it can put its own feelings last from over-giving, or bottle things up unable to speak its mind. Reserved, it is not good at advertising its feelings, but in return the depth of its love and its reassurance are guaranteed.

When You Date

A reassuring partner who supports you from behind with quiet care. Great at giving and wonderfully comfortable. They bottle up feelings, so putting your thanks into words delights them.

When You Fight

Not wanting to make waves, they give in and endure, storing complaints inside. Ask first "are you pushing yourself?" and they open up with relief.

How to Get Closer

Noticing this ones offhand consideration and returning it in words matters most. Each time you say thank you or you really help me, its heart fills with warmth. Bad at voicing its own true feelings, it helps to gently ask what it wants or whether it is overdoing it and draw its feelings out. More than an aggressive approach, creating a calm, reassuring atmosphere is the road to trust. Build a relationship where you properly return the kindness you receive, and this one becomes a partner who stays beside you for life.
